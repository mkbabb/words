## Pattern Codes

### Verbs
- [I] intransitive — "She *laughed*."
- [T] transitive — "He *broke* the window."
- [Tn.pr] + noun + prep — "She *blamed* him for it."
- [Dn.n] ditransitive — "He *gave* her a book."
- [V-ing] + gerund — "I *enjoy* swimming."
- [V to-inf] + infinitive — "They *decided* to leave."
- [V that] + that-clause — "He *claimed* that..."
- [V wh] + wh-clause — "I *wonder* why..."

### Nouns
- [C] countable — "three *ideas*"
- [U] uncountable — "some *information*"
- [sing] singular only — "the *gist*"
- [pl] plural only — "*trousers*"

### Adjectives
- [attrib] attributive only — "the *main* reason" (not ×"the reason is main")
- [pred] predicative only — "the child is *asleep*" (not ×"the asleep child")
- [+prep] takes preposition — "*afraid* of", "*keen* on"
