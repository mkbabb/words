{{ definition }}

## Categories

| Register | Where it appears | Anchors |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| formal | Academic papers, legal documents, official speeches | commence, endeavor, ascertain, notwithstanding |
| neutral | News, textbooks, general conversation | start, try, find out, however |
| informal | Casual speech, texts, social media | gonna, stuff, cool, hang out |
| slang | In-group speech, rapidly changing | slay, salty, sus, vibe check |
| technical | Domain-specific professional contexts | polymorphism, tachycardia, amortization |
| literary | Poetry, elevated prose, archaic usage | hitherto, betwixt, resplendent, erstwhile |

## Decision Rules

1. If the word appears primarily in academic/professional writing → formal.
2. If restricted to a specific professional field → technical (takes precedence over formal).
3. If the word would sound odd in a formal letter → informal or slang.
4. If it would sound odd in casual conversation → formal or literary.
5. If it sounds natural in both → neutral.
6. Literary = archaic, poetic, or deliberately elevated. Not just "fancy."
